"duration": 0.05 , - rotor spin speed
"Lift power": 1.5 , - air resistance level - for large helicopters there should be more
"No power": 700 , - how much the helicopter loses traction compared to Full power
"Full power": 750 , - the maximum height when the helicopter loses its thrust
"side strength": 1.3 , - main rotor erosion
"power before": 1 , - power of the rear rotor
"friction": 0.3 , - friction coefficient
"Mass": 1500.81 , - weight
"incline": 0.5 , - side inclination speed
"front tilt": 0.5 , - tilt in front and back
"inclineMouse": 2.5 , - side inclination speed Mouse
"front tiltMouse": 2.5 , - tilt in front and back Mouse
"m povorot": 0.5 , - rotation speed
///if you turn off all damage, then the helicopter will no longer receive damage from bullets
"Chassis": 0 - 1000 multiplies damage from the earth by the number in the parameter (0 disables) 2 multiplies by 2 - wheels
"Body": 0 - 1000 multiplies damagefrom the earth by number in parameter (0 disables) 2 multiplies by 2 - body
"Engine": 0 - 1000 multiplies damagefrom the earth by number in parameter (0 disables) 2 multiplies by 2 - engine (if the is damaged, it will not be able 
to keep high speeds because of this, the helicopter will not be able to take off and will slowly fall)
"Fuel": 0 - 1000 multiplies the damagefrom the earth by the number in the parameter (0 disables) 2 multiplies by 2 - tank (if damaged then gasoline will decrease rapidly)
"Rotor1": 0 - 1000 multiplies the damagefrom the earth by the number in the parameter (0 disables) 2 multiplies the main rotor by 2
"Rotor2": 0 - 1000 multiplies the damagefrom the earth by the number in the parameter (0 disables) 2 multiplies rotor 2 by 2 (if damaged, the helicopter 
loses control and will spin around its axis)
///if you turn off all damage, then the helicopter will no longer receive damage from bullets
"kill z": 0 , example 0-1- deal damage to players with damage from a blow(off damage 0)
"gasoline costs": 0.001 , - gasoline waste
"gasoline restart": 1 , example 0-1- automatic refueling at spawn
"c camDistance": 10 , example 0.1-100 - distance camera
"maxFuel": 30 , example 0.1-100 - max Fuel
"m Crash dem": 15 , example 0.1-100 - maximum speed at which the sound of Crash
"m engineVolume": 1 , example 0.001-100 - Volume sound engine
"m rotorVolume": 1 , example 0.001-100 - Volume sound rotor
"m warningVolume": 1 , example 0.001-100 - Volume sound warning
"m crashVolume": 1 , example 0.001-100 - Volume sound crash
"config_3rdPerson": 0.0, - if 1 then enable HUD in 1st person
"coef_damage_cont": 1.0, - increase or decrease total damage (multiply)
"coef_damage_weapom": 1.0 - doesn't work yet.
